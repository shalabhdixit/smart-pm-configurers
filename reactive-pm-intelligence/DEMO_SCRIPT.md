## Minute 0:30-1:30

Open the prediction panel. Highlight the 30/60/90-day recurrence risk and explain that interval regularity, frequency, and recency are driving the score.

Click Generate PM Schedules. Show the PM title, due date, frequency bucket, and assigned skill set that appear immediately.

## Minute 3:30-4:30

Move to the savings and business-value views. Call out the 35% savings target, 40% technician efficiency upside, and 28% customer satisfaction uplift opportunity.

Finish on the executive deck and roadmap. Ask for pilot expansion, engineering support, and access to live CAFM data.
