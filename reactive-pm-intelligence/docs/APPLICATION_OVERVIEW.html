Reactive To PM Intelligence

This application transforms historical reactive work orders into a full decision layer: recurring-pattern visibility, predictive failure risk, PM schedule recommendations, and a leadership-ready operating narrative through the dashboard and PM Copilot.

Live dashboard PM Copilot PM automation

What Makes It Different

E2E

The solution does not stop at analytics. It carries the workflow from raw signal to prioritized PM action and executive storytelling.

Signal

Reactive work orders

CAFM-like failure history provides the raw pattern substrate.

Insight

Recurring-pattern detection

Grouped signature analysis isolates repeatable breakdown behavior.

Prediction

30/60/90 day risk

Recurrence scoring prioritizes what is most likely to fail again soon.

Action

PM conversion

High-risk patterns become planning-ready preventive schedules.

Primary Personas

  • SLT and account leadership needing a quantified value story.
  • Facilities managers needing hotspot and risk visibility.
  • PM planners needing a ranked, explainable backlog.
  • Operational teams needing a cleaner move from reactive noise to planned execution.

User Outcomes

  • See the most exposed facilities and assets instantly.
  • Understand which patterns are recurring and why they matter.
  • Generate PM schedules without manual back-office synthesis.
  • Use the assistant for SLT summaries, risk framing, and action guidance.
